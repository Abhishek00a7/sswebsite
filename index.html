I am a

Shambhavi Sharma

A dancer, artist and changemaker who blends creativity with purpose. A dedicated Kuchipudi performer and painter, she channels her talents into Dance for a Cause and Art for a Cause, using classical arts to heal, inspire, and empower. She is currently studying Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Economics at Sanskriti School, Delhi, balancing her academic aspirations with her artistic pursuits.

About Me

As a student at Sanskriti School, Delhi, I have been weaving stories through Kuchipudi for nearly a decade. Trained under the guidance of my revered gurus, Raja Radha Reddy, I have mastered the intricate expressions and rhythms of this classical dance form, celebrating India’s rich cultural heritage. From performing at Rajgir Mahotsav and the Parampara Series to using dance as a therapeutic tool for healing patients at R and R Hospital, I see dance as more than an art—it is a way to connect, inspire, and heal.

Nrityamrit

Nrityamrit—a confluence of rhythm, devotion, and purpose—is Shambhavi Sharma’s artistic expression, where movement transcends mere performance. Through the flowing grace of Kuchipudi, she breathes life into stories, weaving emotions into motion. Nrityamrit is her way of embracing dance as a sacred offering, a vessel of healing, and a call to inspire. With every step, she seeks to stir souls, to bridge tradition with transformation, and to let the essence of art flow like nectar—soothing, empowering, and eternal.

Dance At a Glance

Rajgir Mahotsav, Nalanda University

I performed at Rajgir Mahotsav 2022, showcasing my Kuchipudi skills under the guidance of my revered gurus, Raja Radha Reddy. My performance was dedicated to the 'Jal Jeevan Hariyali' campaign, celebrating the state's commitment to sustainability. This experience not only boosted my confidence but also deepened my appreciation for India’s rich cultural heritage. During the festival, I had the opportunity to explore the historic ruins of Nalanda University, further enriching my connection with Bihar’s profound legacy.

G20 University Connect

As part of the G20 University Connect initiative, I performed Kuchipudi, representing India’s vibrant cultural heritage on a global platform. It was an honor to share the grace and storytelling of this classical art form, showcasing its timeless beauty to a diverse audience.

Parampara Series at Kamani Auditorium

I had the honor of performing at the prestigious Parampara Series 2024 at Kamani Auditorium, New Delhi. Under the guidance of my revered gurus, Raja Radha Reddy, I showcased my Kuchipudi artistry, blending tradition with elegance. The event celebrated the essence of classical arts and the Guru-Shishya Parampara, with Hon’ble Union Culture Minister Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat gracing the occasion as the Chief Guest.

Natya Taringini

I had the privilege of performing at 48th Anniversary celebration of Natya Tarangini, held at Raja Radha Rangmanch in Delhi. Under the guidance of my gurus, Raja Radha Reddy, I presented an exquisite Kuchipudi recital, paying tribute to the timeless traditions of classical dance. It was a moment of deep reverence and joy, celebrating the legacy of this divine art form.

School cultural events and international showcases

As a PCM student, I explored the intersection of Physics and Dance Choreography, blending science with movement to create a unique form of expression. Stepping beyond traditional boundaries, I choreographed performances that translated scientific concepts into graceful dance movements, making Physics more visual, dynamic, and engaging.

Strings and Steps Youth Festival

I participated in 3rd Strings N Steps Youth Festival – Parampara 2019, held at Andhra Bhawan Auditorium, New Delhi. Presented by Strings 'N' Steps Academy in collaboration with Andhra Pradesh Bhawan and Payal Foundation, the event was a beautiful celebration of India’s classical arts. It was an enriching experience to perform alongside talented artists, honoring the legacy of our cultural heritage.

Leadership & Public Speaking

Model United Nations (MUN):

Model United Nations has been my gateway to diplomacy, leadership, and global discourse. From representing nations on issues like climate change and security to leading as Director General of INTER SMUN 2024, each conference has sharpened my ability to debate, negotiate, and inspire change. Whether engaging with international delegates at Harvard Model United Nations (HMUN) 2024, earning recognition at Strawberry Fields MUN, or winning awards for research and analysis, MUN has fueled my passion for problem-solving and global awareness.

Udanify:

A step towards menstrual hygiene awareness

Udanify has been my platform to drive awareness, empowerment, and change. As a fundraiser and advocate, I work to provide adolescent girls with accurate menstrual hygiene education, ensuring they have the knowledge and resources to manage menstruation with dignity. Through community engagement and outreach, I strive to break taboos, foster open conversations, and support Udanify’s mission of empowering women through informed choices.

Chakra Innovations

A step towards menstrual hygiene awareness

Chakra Innovations has been my avenue to explore the intersection of technology and social impact. Engaging with innovative solutions, I contribute to projects that drive sustainability, accessibility, and positive change. By integrating creativity with problem solving, I aim to support meaningful advancements that empower communities and shape a better future.

Unruly Art

Unruly Art allows me to use art as a tool for inclusion and self-expression. Collaborating with specially-abled students, I help them discover confidence through colors and creativity, proving that art knows no barriers. Through this initiative, I strive to make art a medium of empowerment, breaking limitations and celebrating diverse abilities.
